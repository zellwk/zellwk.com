We rarely see the full picture.

And we project on the world with our coloured lenses, based on what we believe to be true.

But the good news is **our beliefs are just stories**.

- They're stories told to us.
- Or stories we create.

Which means it's possible to invent another story that allows you to see, receive, and even accept what we are rejecting.

Because **what we reject usually comes from an angle we don't see**.

Conjuring a new point of view is an exercise in opening our minds and, with curiosity, ask: "What's happening? Why does this happen?"

**We begin there**.

And soon, we see the world in a different light.
