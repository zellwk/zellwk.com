Caterpillars need to go through a metamorphosis to become a butterfly.

Hermit crabs need to look for new shells as they grow.

{/* more */}

Humans undergo struggle, difficulties and stress in order to grow.

It might be that this stress and struggle is necessary for one to live up to their fullest potential.

So, why do you reject forwarding stress?
