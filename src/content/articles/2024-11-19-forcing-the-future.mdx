The future you want doesn't happen just because you try to force it to be.

{/* more */}

Piling on pressure and stress can help with manifestation to a certain extent. Beyond that, there's diminishing — eventually negative — returns.

The best you can do is live your best in every moment. The future will manifest the prayers you put into the world with each breath you take.
